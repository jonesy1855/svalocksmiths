Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

Shropshire Vehicle Access ("we", "our", "us") operates as a mobile auto locksmith service provider. For the purposes of UK GDPR regulations, we act as the data controller for any personal details submitted via our digital service desk platform.

2. Information We Collect & Why

When you fill out an inquiry or emergency dispatch form on our website, we collect your Name, Contact Details (Phone/Email), and Location/Vehicle Details. This data is processed strictly under the lawful basis of contractual necessity—we require this information to coordinate our mobile workshop and dispatch a locksmith to your location.

3. Data Processor & Storage

Your data is securely routed via our form architecture processor, Formspree, and lands directly into our secure Google Workspace email environment. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your private contact details to external third-party marketing companies.

4. Cookie Disclosures

Our website is built using static architectural code and does not deploy tracking scripts, advertising pixels, or profiling cookies. The only cookies utilized are strictly operational, system-essential tags used by our form processor to protect the server from automated spam submissions.
