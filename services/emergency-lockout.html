What is "Non-Destructive" Entry?

Non-destructive opening means gaining access to your vehicle without causing any physical damage to the lock cylinder, doors, bodywork, or glass. We do not use crude force, pry bars, or generic wire hangers that bend door frames and ruin weather seals.

How We Work: Technical Precision

Instead of forcing the door, we decode and pick the lock mechanically using precision diagnostic tools called Lishi picks. Each tool is designed specifically for a particular vehicle make and model (such as HU66 for VW/Audi or HU101 for Ford).

By inserting these specialist picks directly into the keyway, we manipulate the internal wafers one by one—exactly as a real laser-cut key would. Once all wafers are picked to their correct positions, the lock rotates cleanly, clicking open safely and leaving the mechanism in perfect working order.

⚠️ Note on Deadlocked Vehicles: Most modern cars and commercial vans use "double-locking" or deadlocking. When active, pulling the interior door handles or pushing the central locking buttons on the dashboard will not unlock the vehicle. Mechanical picking via the drivers door lock is the only safe way to bypass a deadlock state.
