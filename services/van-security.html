Securing Local Shropshire Tradesmen

Factory-fitted van locks are continuously targeted by sophisticated thieves using methods like 'peel and steal' or specialized skeleton keys. Upgrading your commercial fleet with secondary mechanical locks is the only true way to protect your livelihood and tool inventory.

What We Are Fleshing Out:

  • Heavy Duty Deadlocks: Brass-cylinder mechanical hookbolts installed directly into the door chassis, acting entirely independent of the vehicle's electrical central locking network.
  • Slamlocks: Ideal for multi-drop delivery drivers, automatically locking the commercial door securely shut the second you swing it closed.
  • Internal Shielding Plates: Outer skin reinforcing structural steel plates designed to physically stop thieves piercing panels to clip internal locking rods.
